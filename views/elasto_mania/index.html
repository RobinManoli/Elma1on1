Elasto Mania

Elasto Mania is a very unique game. It has a game play precision and fine tuning which is beyond what is possible in most other games. It is also very dynamic, where new tricks are found over time, year after year.

Behold this rec (replay) sequence. This powerful combination of moves is not pre-programmed into the game, such as L+L+R+R+A+A+B. No, to make a moves like these, one needs pure skill, such a skill that takes a lot of practice.

Here is a couple of examples of how elastic the bike is. Combined with a world of vector based levels (tracks), this calls for a very broad possibility of play.

Riders Spef, Zero, Nekit, Madness, FANTomck's, Bjenn and Zweq illustrate the power of this game.
